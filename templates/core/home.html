Learn about blood donation

Who can donate?
  • Healthy adults from 18 to 65 years old
  • Minimum weight 50 kg (110 lb)
  • Normal hemoglobin levels
How often?

Whole blood every 56 days. Platelets every 7–14 days depending on local guidance.

Not today if...
  • You have a fever or flu-like symptoms
  • You recently had surgery or a major dental procedure
  • You are pregnant or recently postpartum
